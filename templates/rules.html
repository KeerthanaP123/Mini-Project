General Rules

    • Strict silence, discipline must be maintained in the library. Misuse of cell phone is not allowed.
    • Smoking, eating, sleeping and talking loudly are strictly prohibited in the library.
    • Documents taken out of the shelves must be left on the table. Replacing the books on shelves is not encouraged as they may get misplaced. A misplaced book is like a lost book.
    • A non-member can use the library material on the premises with the permission of the Librarian.
    • Readers should not mark, underline, dog-ear, write, tear pages or otherwise damage the library documents.
    • Newspapers, magazines and journals must be read-only in the library on specific tables and should not be taken to any other reading areas.
    • No library material can be taken out of the library without permission. Unauthorized removal of anything belonging to the library will be treated as theft and dealt with accordingly.
    • Anyone who violates the rules and regulations of the library would be liable to lose the privilege of library membership and may be debarred from using the library facilities.
    • Suggestions on all aspects, of library services, are welcome.

Book Circulation Rules

    • General Books are circulated for 15 days.
    • Issued books can renew to 15 days more to members only if it is not reserved by any other member.
    • There will be a fine of ₹ 0.25 per day for the late books. The minimum fine will be ₹ 1.
    • If the issued book is lost or damaged by users, A new copy of the same edition or current edition needs to buy back to the library by the user. If not, the member should pay a fine of 4 times of publication price of that book.
    • The library will issue member ID cards free of cost. If this card is lost, there will be a fee for issuing a new one.
    • When a reserved book is returned by a member, notification will be sent to that member via E-Mail.
    • If any member has not returned an issued book for more than 6 months, the member should give a satisfactory explanation to the library. Otherwise, the membership of those users will be cancelled without any further notice and legal action will take as per the Public Library Act.
    • Members can use the reference book in the reference section with the permission of the reference librarian. Reference books will not be issued.
    • If members need a copy of any books part, members can use reprography services within the reference section of the library.
    • Suggestions on all aspects of library services, are welcome.
